About

What is VFree?

VFree is a VTuber group designed for ultimate freedom. No auditions, no schedules, and no contracts—just pure creativity on your terms.

Welcome to 『VFree』, the Vtuber group built for dreamers, creators, and anyone who craves ultimate freedom in their journey! No auditions. No contracts. No rigid schedules—just a supportive space where you can truly be yourself.

Why『VFree』?

At 『VFree』, we believe that creativity thrives when given the freedom to grow. That’s why we keep it simple:

No auditions – No stressful screening processes. Just be yourself!
No contracts or requirements – No restrictions, obligations, or pressure—just pure freedom.
Your own schedule & freedom – Stream, create, and engage at your own pace.

How to join

Joining 『VFree』 is as easy as saying “I’m in!” There are no applications, no formal process, and no limitations. Simply state that you're part of the group wherever you’d like—on your socials, streams, or content. You can also leave at any time with no hassle. You’re in control.

A Community for Creators

Whether you're an aspiring Vtuber, a seasoned creator, or just exploring, 『VFree』 is here to encourage and celebrate your journey. There’s no hierarchy.
